How a Baton Rouge accounting firm reduced email compromise risk before tax season.

If one employee inbox was compromised, hundreds of client tax records could be exposed before anyone noticed.

Cyber Hound strengthened MFA, Conditional Access, admin protection, email security, external forwarding restrictions, SharePoint and OneDrive sharing controls, suspicious login monitoring, incident response, executive reporting, and 24/7 monitoring.

RiskClient tax records, payroll files, SSNs, and financial documents.
OutcomeStronger account protection and clearer response before peak season.
